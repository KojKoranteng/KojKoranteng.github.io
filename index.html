How to play

  1. Choose from the times tables above like a boss.
  2. Put on your space suit & enter into the game arena.
  3. Read the question above the game board carefully.
  4. Click the question mark where the answer should be.

Your mission

  1. Collect all nine space trophies for your cabinet.
  2. The first trophy appears after a few correct answers.
  3. Use "Fifty-Fifty" or "Narrow down" to assist you.
  4. Do use them wisely. They vanish after too many uses.
  5. Good luck Space Cadet! We're counting on you!
